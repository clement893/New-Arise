## Interpreting Your Results

### If most scores are 5-10 (Foundation):
**Priority:** Build foundational habits across all areas
**Timeline:** 3-6 months to establish basics
**Support:** Consider professional guidance or support groups

### If most scores are 11-15 (Developing):
**Priority:** Strengthen consistency and refine habits
**Timeline:** 2-4 months to solidify practices
**Support:** Use tracking tools and accountability partners

### If most scores are 16-20 (Strong):
**Priority:** Optimize performance and maintain habits
**Timeline:** Ongoing optimization and refinement
**Support:** Consider advanced training or coaching

### If most scores are 21-25 (Optimal):
**Priority:** Share knowledge and support others
**Timeline:** Ongoing leadership and mentorship
**Support:** Lead initiatives and mentor others

## Action Planning Tips

1. **Start with your lowest-scoring pillar**
   - This will have the biggest impact on overall wellness
   - Follow the specific actions for that score range

2. **Don't try to change everything at once**
   - Focus on 1-2 pillars at a time
   - Build momentum with small wins

3. **Celebrate progress, not perfection**
   - Moving from Foundation to Developing is a major achievement
   - Each level represents significant improvement

4. **Reassess regularly**
   - Take the assessment every 3-6 months
   - Track your progress over time
   - Adjust strategies based on results

5. **Use your strengths to support your growth areas**
   - If you're strong in stress management, use those skills for sleep
   - Strong social connections can support movement goals
